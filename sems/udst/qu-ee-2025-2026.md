# Core Curriculum Program (33 credit hours)  

A minimum of 33 credit hours are required to complete the Core Curriculum Program as detailed below.  

# Identity and Communication package (15 CH)  

The number of credit hours required for this package is 15 credit hours. The specific courses to be completed by students are:  

- ARAB 100 Arabic I
- ENGL 202 English Language I – Post Foundation
- ENGL 203 English Language II – Post Foundation
- DAWA 111 Islamic Culture
- HIST 121 History of Qatar
